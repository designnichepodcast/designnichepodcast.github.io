About the host

Hi, I’m Jacob Ottesen, a UX designer and wanna-be podcast creator. I studied Web Design & Development at Brigham Young University–Idaho and now work at IBM in Austin, TX. The team I work on is building a design system for all experiences on IBM.com.

Why the podcast?

I love learning and having side projects to fill my time outside of my day-to-day work. I’ll admit I selfishly started the podcast so I could learn from cool designers and archive my learnings, but I also hope listeners can gain something valuable from each discussion.
